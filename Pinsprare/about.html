About Pinspirare

Pinspirare has been created as a share tool to allow individuals to "give" or "offer" inspirational phrases to others. The website then offering the ability for its participants to provide and reap comfort, solace, or guidance through a well-structured collection of words succinctly presented.

The name is a derived from the combination of two words "Pin" and "spirare". A "Pin" (commonly known as a badge in the UK) is synonymous for sharing information, advertising or displaying a symbol or emblem. Spirare is a Latin word for breathe but its foundation is assimilated to "pulling together". Pinspirare is therefore used to suggest an open display of encouragement or inspiration.

The specific intent of the site is to give and receive freely and it seems appropriate to use a quote to reinforce its objective: "If you light a lamp for someone else it will also brighten your path"

~Buddha
